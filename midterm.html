Palo Alto Library Used Book Sale

Welcome! Come visit the library to view a selection of over 50,000 items at our monthly book sale.

When: Saturday's and Sunday's 8:00am-1:00pm PT

Where: Palo Alto Library

Cost of Books: Varies depending on room. See more info below.

Main Room

  • Hardback Books: $1+ each
  • Paperback Books: 50+ cents each
  • Bring large book donations to the Main Room.

New Room

  • Children's books, music, videos, and toys.
  • Picture books, school age fiction & nonfiction, non-English language books, CDs and DVDs
  • Books for parents & teachers.

Bargain Books: Rooms K6 and K7

  • Hardback Books: All $1 each
  • Paperback Books: All 50 cents each
  • Sunday's: Half off prices!
  • Saturday's: Opens at 7:00am

Health & Safety

  • If you arrive before 8am, you will be given 2 tickets max per person to reserve your spot in line.
  • From 8am-11am, you will be admitted into the Main Room based on your ticket number.
  • Only 160 customers are allowed in the Main Room at a time.
  • Max 12 books for purchase. From there, please go to the checkout line. You may then re-join the line outside to enter the Main Room.
